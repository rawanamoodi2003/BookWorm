The Number Three Classic #3

Holes

Louis Sachar

3.00
Category: Classic

Stanley Yelnats is under a curse. A curse that began with his no-good-dirty-rotten-pig-stealing-great-great-grandfather and has since followed generations of Yelnats. Now Stanley has been unjustly sent to a boys’ detention center, Camp Green Lake, where the boys build character by spending all day, every day digging holes exactly five feet wide and five feet deep. There is no lake at Camp Green Lake. But there are an awful lot of holes.

Language: English

272 pages

About The
Author

Louis Sachar

Louis Sachar is a renowned American author, best known for his award-winning book "Holes." Born on March 20, 1954, in East Meadow, New York, Sachar initially pursued law as a career but later shifted his focus to writing. His breakthrough came with "Holes," which won the prestigious Newbery Medal in 1999 and was adapted into a successful film.
