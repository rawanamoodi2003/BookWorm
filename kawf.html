The Sheikh's Challenge: Unveiling the Second World

One day a discussion began with the sheikh. The sheikh asked him whether he believed in the second world or not. About The
Author

Osamah_Al Muslim

Osama Al-Muslim is a Saudi writer and novelist, distinguished by his unique literary style and his ability to explore social and psychological aspects in his works. He is considered one of the writers who have gained wide fame in the Arab world, with his writings ranging from novels to short stories. In his books, he addresses various issues related to cultural identity, social transformations, and human relationships in a deep and inspiring manner. Thanks to his creativity and the diversity of his themes, Osama Al-Muslim is regarded as one of the creative writers contributing to enriching contemporary Arabic literature.
