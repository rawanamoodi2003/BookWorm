Batman: Year One

Bruce Wayne returns to Gotham after years of training abroad and adopts the persona of Batman to fight crime and corruption in his city. About The
Author

Frank Miller

Frank Miller is a renowned comic book writer, artist, and film director, known for his gritty and noir-influenced storytelling style. He rose to prominence in the 1980s with his groundbreaking work on titles such as "Daredevil" and "Batman," redefining the way these characters were portrayed in comics.
