The Number Three Horror #3

Youth voices in hotels

Arnaldur Andreasson

3.02
Category: Horror

A corpse in the hotel RÖDDIN (VOICES) Arnaldur Andreasson introduces us into the world of mysterious crime, through a complex criminal plot;A few days before Christmas, Gudlau Gur, a hotel guard in Reykjavík who sometimes plays Santa, is found disguised as Santa Claus. Stabbed to death in his miserable little room in the hotel where he worked, in a shameful situation. It soon becomes clear that the staff and guests have something to hide. But the most mysterious secrets are buried with the victim.
